The Dashboard is command central for your site. You can bring up the Dashboard at any time by clicking the Tendenci icon in the top left corner of your screen. (You must be logged in to see the icon.)

Icons

Each icon on the Dashboard represents a module that controls data or settings for your site.

For example, if you click Users, you will have the option to:

  • Search your User database,
  • Add new Users,
  • Edit User records and reset passwords, and
  • Control site reflexes associated with your Users, such as whether or not New Users are automatically signed up for your newsletter. 

Dashboard Nav Menu

Take a moment to run your mouse over the navigation bar at the top of your screen. As an alternative to using the Dashboard icons, you will find quick access to all of the things you will manage as site administrator. 

Tech Support

And don’t miss the links to “Search Helpfiles” and “E-mail Support” at the very bottom left.

The Tendenci help files are a great way to find answers to specific questions about using Tendenci. If you can’t find an answer there, contact our Support team!
