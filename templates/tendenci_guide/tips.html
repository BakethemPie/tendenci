Here are a few parting tips:

  1. Keep your content fresh! Add new articles, photos and stories regularly. Or start a blog on the site! Encourage multiple members of your organization to publish content on the site.
  2. Use photos! People love to see themselves on the site. This is a great way to show how much fun you had at your last event and encourage people to come to the next one!

  3. Make money off your Jobs Listings! Encouraging companies to list job openings in your database is not only a great service for your members, but can be a bigger money maker too! (We didn't cover this one but you will find the interface very similar.)

  4. Use the Help Files! Our users have built hundreds of help files on using Tendenci features, from very specific reflexes to addressing larger concepts. If you can't find it in the Tendenci Help Files, contact our Support Team!
